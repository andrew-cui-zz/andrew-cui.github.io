Hi, I'm Andrew.

I'm a rising junior at the University of Pennsylvania's Fisher Management and Technology (M&T) program from upstate New York, interested in a blend of data and business strategy. Specifically, I'm interested in applying data and analytic skills to understand human behavior (such as in a marketing or sports data context).

I'm also a Tri-Chair for Asian Pacific American Heritage Week 2018, and a computer science teaching assistant.
